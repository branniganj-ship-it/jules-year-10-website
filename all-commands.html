all commands

Donut SMP commands focus on economy, teleportation, and player management, featuring key commands like /ah (auction house), /sell, /rtp (random teleport), and /home to navigate the server. Essential commands include /tpa for player requests, /shop for buying items, and /bal to check currency. tp: Randomly teleport to the Overworld, Nether, or End. /home / /sethome / /delhome: Teleport to, set, or delete your designated home location. /spawn / /hub / /lobby: Teleport back to the main server spawn. /tpa [player]: Request to teleport to another player. /tpaccept / /tpdeny: Accept or decline incoming teleport requests. /warp [name]: Move to specific areas, such as /warp shop, /warp pvp, or /warp farm.

/ah / /a: Opens the Auction House to buy items. /ah sell [price] [amount]: Sells the item in hand on the auction house. /sell: Instantly sells items to the server. /bal: Displays your current money balance. /shop: Opens the GUI for in-game shops.

Player & Team Management: /afk: Enters the AFK zone to earn rewards. /team create [name]: Creates a new player team. /team invite [player]: Invites a player to your team. /list: Shows all players currently online. /msg [player] [message]: Private messages a player. /nightvision: Toggles infinite night vision.

Utility & Info: /help: Displays available commands. /rules: Displays server rules. /bal top: Shows the richest players. /kit: Opens the kit menu to claim items.
